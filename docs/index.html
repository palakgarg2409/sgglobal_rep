Welcome to SG Global, your trusted partner in international food trading!

Founded by Rajiv Garg, SG Global is a dynamic trading firm based in India, boasting an extensive network of suppliers in the Indian Consumer Market. With a focus on quality and innovation, we specialize in the international trade of food products, both processed and unprocessed, originating from the rich landscapes of India.

At SG Global, we are committed to offering our clients access to a diverse range of premium food products that cater to the evolving needs of consumers worldwide. From nutritious superfoods to indulgent snacks, our carefully curated selection ensures satisfaction for every palate.

Our flagship product, Foxnut (Fuchsnuss or Popped Lotus Seeds), stands out as a potentially nutritious delight for global consumers. Packed with antioxidants, high protein, and fiber content, these gluten-free nuts cater to the needs of vegetarians and vegans alike. Available in an array of enticing flavors, they offer a wholesome and healthy alternative to traditional snacks.

In addition to Foxnuts, our product portfolio includes a variety of nutrient-packed seeds such as Chia Seeds, Pumpkin Seeds, Quinoa Seeds, Flax Seeds, Sesame Seeds, and more. These ingredients have become essential components of modern diets, commonly used in making healthy smoothies, oatmeals, and baked goods.

Furthermore, we offer a premium selection of dry fruits, including Almonds, Cashews, Raisins, and more, which can be customized according to our clients' specific needs and preferences.

At SG Global, we understand the importance of personalized service and aim to provide exceptional customer support at every step of the trading process. With a dedicated point of contact based in Wolfsburg, Germany, we ensure seamless communication and assistance for our valued clients in Europe.

Whether you are a distributor, retailer, or importer looking to expand your product offerings, SG Global is here to help. Explore our comprehensive product portfolio and discover the exceptional quality and versatility of Indian food products.
